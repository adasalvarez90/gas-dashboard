# Commission System

The platform distributes commissions generated by investor capital.

Commissions are generated when a tranche becomes fully funded.

---

# Commission Schemes

Two schemes exist:


Scheme A
Scheme B


---

# Scheme A

Total commission = **9%**

Structure:


4% immediate
5% recurring


Example:


Investment = 100,000
Immediate = 4,000
Recurring = 5,000


Recurring commission is divided across the contract duration.

If the tranche is an annex, the recurring commission must be divided across **remaining months**.

---

# Scheme B

Structure:


4% immediate
Remaining paid at month 6


Special rule:

If annex occurs before month 6 → first payment indediate 4% and then remaining payment happens at month 6.

If annex occurs after month 6 → first payment indediate 4% and then remaining payment happens at month 12.

---

# Commission Generation

When a tranche becomes funded:

The system must generate all future commission payments immediately.

This ensures financial consistency and auditability.

These payments are stored as CommissionPayment records.
