# AI Context — Investment Contract & Commission System

The project documentation is written in English.

Developers may interact with the AI in Spanish or English.

The AI should always prioritize the rules defined in this documentation regardless of the language used in the prompt.

This project implements an investment contract management system with commission distribution.

The system is built with:

- Angular
- Ionic
- TypeScript
- Firebase Firestore
- NgRx for state management

The architecture separates **domain logic (engines)** from **data persistence (Firestore services)**.

AI assistants must follow the rules defined in this document when generating code.

# Core Domain Model

The system manages investment contracts and the commissions generated by them.

Main entities:

- Contract
- Tranche
- Deposit
- CommissionPayment
- CommissionConfig
- Advisor

---

# Contracts

A contract represents an agreement with an investor.

Key characteristics:

- Contract duration is always **12 months**
- The contract **does NOT start when signed**
- The contract starts **when the first tranche becomes fully funded**

Important fields:

contract.uid
contract.scheme ("A" or "B")
contract.source
contract.roles
contract.startDate
contract.endDate

A contract may receive additional capital contributions during its lifetime.

These contributions are represented as **tranches**.

---

# Tranches

A **tranche** represents a capital contribution to a contract.

Example:

Investor commits:

100,000 MXN

Later adds:

50,000 MXN

This creates:

Tranche 1 → 100,000  
Tranche 2 → 50,000

Tranche fields:

contractUid
amount
totalDeposited
funded
fundedAt
sequence


Rules:

- Deposits accumulate in the tranche
- When `totalDeposited >= amount` the tranche becomes **funded**
- When a tranche becomes funded, **commission obligations are generated**

Sequence represents the order:


1 → initial investment
2 → annex
3 → annex


---

# Deposits

Deposits represent money transfers from the investor.

Deposits fund a tranche.

Rules:

- Deposits increase `totalDeposited`
- Deposits cannot exceed tranche amount
- Deposits may occur across multiple transfers
- When the tranche reaches the required amount it becomes funded

Example:

Tranche = 100,000

Deposits:

40,000  
40,000  
20,000

Now:


totalDeposited = 100,000
funded = true
fundedAt = timestamp


At this moment the commission engine must run.

# Advisors and Roles

Each contract assigns advisors to specific roles.

Roles:


CONSULTANT
KAM
MANAGER
SALES_DIRECTION
OPERATIONS
CEO
REFERRAL (optional)


The REFERRAL role only exists when the contract source is `REFERIDORA`.

Each role receives a percentage of the total commission.

These percentages are defined in **CommissionConfig**.

Example:


CONSULTANT → 33.25%
CEO → 48.45%
KAM → 5%


These values depend on the **investment source**.

---

# Commission Schemes

There are two commission schemes.


Scheme A
Scheme B


---

# Scheme A

Total commission = **9% of tranche capital**

Distribution:


4% immediate
5% recurring


Example:

Investment = 100,000


Immediate commission = 4,000
Recurring commission = 5,000


Recurring commission is divided across **months of the contract**.

If the tranche is the initial tranche:


5,000 / 12 months


If the tranche is an annex contribution, the recurring commission must be divided across **remaining months of the contract**.

Example:

Annex in month 3.

Remaining months:


12 - 3 = 9 months


Recurring commission:


5,000 / 9


---

# Scheme B

4% commission **immediate**. The **extra** (depending on yield) is paid in the **sixth month of the contract term** (vigencia del contrato).

Structure:

- 4% immediate
- Remaining commission (extra by yield) paid at **month 6** of the contract term

Annex rule:

- **Annex before month 6** of the contract term: 4% immediate; extra (by yield) is paid at **month 6** (together with any other due at that date).
- **Annex from month 6 onward** of the contract term: 4% immediate; remaining (by yield) is paid at **month 12** of the contract term.

# Commission Generation Strategy

When a tranche becomes **fully funded**, the system must generate **ALL commission obligations immediately**.

Example:

A tranche becomes funded.

The system generates:


Immediate commission
Future recurring commissions
Future scheduled commissions


All payments are stored as `CommissionPayment` records.

This guarantees:

- financial auditability
- stable accounting
- deterministic calculations

The engine must never recalculate historical commissions.

# Monthly Definition

The system defines months using **monthly anniversary logic**.

Example:

Start date:


January 15


Next months:


February 15
March 15
April 15


Edge case:


January 30 → February 28 or 29


---

# Commission Cut Dates

Commission payments are processed twice per month.

Cut days:


7
21


Rules:


If dueDate ≤ 7 → paid in 7 cut
If dueDate ≤ 21 → paid in 21 cut
If after 21 → next cycle


---

# Contract Cancellation

Contracts may be cancelled.

If a contract is cancelled:

- future unpaid commissions must be marked as `cancelled`
- past paid commissions remain unchanged

---

# Architecture Rules

Domain calculations must exist in:


/domain/engines


Examples:


commission-engine.service.ts
role-resolver.service.ts


These services must **never interact with Firestore**.

They only perform calculations.

Persistence happens in Firestore services.

---

# Important Implementation Rule

When generating commissions for a tranche:

1. Resolve role splits
2. Calculate total commission
3. Generate CommissionPaymentDraft records
4. Persist them as CommissionPayment records

The engine must always be deterministic and pure.
